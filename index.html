Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favourite foods

Ingredient

  • 2 eggs
  • butter
  • cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs
